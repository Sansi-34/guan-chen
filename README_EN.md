## The Genesis

**"To those deeply entangled in psychological turmoil: May you recognize your own radiance and live out your true value."**

Observe the dust, perceive the universe.
We often lose ourselves in pleasing the world and conforming to external standards, leading to deep spiritual exhaustion or inner wounds. We criticize ourselves, fixated on our "flaws," unaware that the crack is where the light enters.

Do not perceive this as a cold "machine" or an aloof "expert."  
**"See the Dust" exists solely to be a non-judgmental mirror when you feel worthless or trapped in the night. Crossing the boundaries of East and West, using BaZi, Ziwei, and MBTI as symbols, it catches all your vulnerabilities and illuminates the brilliance you've always possessed.**
